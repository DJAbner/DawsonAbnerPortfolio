EH-Participation-Tracker

Project for Software Engineering Course at Texas A&M (September 2020-December 2020)

Web application used by the Engineering Honors program at Texas A&M for tracking members and their participation in events

What I Worked On

  • Created CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionality for Engineering Honors Events Page
  • Created Export and Import functionalities for all important data
  • Helped with Documentation and Handoff of the product

What Went Well

  • Frequent communication and peer coding allowed us to get our work done efficently and correctly
  • Use of Ruby on Rails made the implementation of many functionalities simple and robust
  • Weekly standup meetings and client demos made transfer of the final product quick and efficent

What Didn't Go Well/What I learned

  • Unclear and changing scope requirements led to some wasted work and effort
  • Poor understanding of Ruby on Rails led to slow development startup
  • Involvement in other classes and projects caused oversights when working on this project
