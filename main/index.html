Howdy! My Name is Dawson Abner and I am a senior Computer Science student at Texas A&M University, minoring in Game Design and Development. My hobbies include working out, dancing, and reading.

Because I have been playing video games since I was a child, I've taken a deep interest in game development, which led me to pursue the major and minor that I am. However, throughout college I have gotten more involved with Esports, specifically Super Smash Brothers, and all of the work that goes into it behind the scenes. This has led me to pursue a career in web and software development, so that I am able to work on fulfilling projects that will help the Esports ecosystem grow.

By joining Texas A&M Esports (an on-campus organization centered around competing in and running events for a variety of video games), I have been able to explore my interest in Esports further by getting involved with a diverse and welcoming community. Not only was I able to compete on the Super Smash Brothers Ultimate Collegiate Team, but I also had the opportunity of becoming an Team Lead for the Super Smash Brothers Ultimate division. This allowed me to learn more about running events and moderating communities as I helped administrate in person events, online events, and community chat rooms like Discord. I have also worked as an Intramural Esports Assistant for Texas A&M Rec Sports, where I help to create Esports events and foster a diverse community for the entire university.
